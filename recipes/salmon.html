Salmon

Nutrition Facts

515 calories; protein 39.2g; carbohydrates 29.7g; fat 25.5g; cholesterol 111.7mg; sodium 525.4mg.

Ingredients

  • ½ cup maple syrup
  • ¼ cup Dijon mustard
  • ½ teaspoon minced fresh thyme
  • ¼ teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 2 pounds salmon fillets
  • kosher salt and ground black pepper to taste
  • 1 tablespoon canola oil, or as needed

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 450 degrees F (230 degrees C).
  2. Combine maple syrup, Dijon mustard, thyme, and cinnamon in a small bowl.
  3. Season salmon with salt and pepper. Heat canola oil in a large oven-proof saute pan over medium heat. Cook salmon until browned, about 3 minutes. Flip and brush surface generously with maple syrup mixture. Cook until bottom is browned, about 3 minutes more.
  4. Transfer to the preheated oven and bake until salmon flakes easily with a fork, 5 to 10 minutes more.
